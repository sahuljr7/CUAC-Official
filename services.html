Research

The Astronomy Club’s Research Section aims to foster an environment of intellectual curiosity and scientific inquiry. We strive to contribute to the global astronomical community while promoting a deeper understanding and appreciation of the universe among our fellow students and the wider public.


At Chandigarh University’s Astronomy Club, we believe that the night sky holds the key to unlocking the secrets of our existence. Our research endeavors are driven by curiosity, a thirst for discovery, and a deep-seated fascination with the stars, planets, galaxies, and everything that lies beyond our planet Earth.

Our dedicated team of astrophiles, amateur astronomers, and aspiring scientists work tirelessly to engage in cutting-edge research projects and observations. Through collaboration and a shared passion for astronomy, we explore various facets of the universe, including:

Stellar Evolution

Investigates the life cycle of stars, from their formation in stellar nurseries to their eventual demise as supernovae, neutron stars, or black holes.

Exoplanetary Studies

Focuses on the discovery, characterization, and study of planets outside our solar system, known as exoplanets.

Galactic Dynamics

Examines the structure, dynamics, and evolution of galaxies, including our own Milky Way and external galaxies across the universe.

Cosmology

Explores the origin, structure, and evolution of the universe as a whole, from the Big Bang to its current state.

Astrobiology

Examines the potential for life beyond Earth by studying the conditions and environments that could support life elsewhere in the universe.
